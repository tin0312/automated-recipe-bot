## Overview

### The challenge

Users should be able to:

- Search for their dishes by keyword.
- Search for their dishes by descriptions.
- Search for their dishes in any language.
- Keep track of what they tried.
- See the dish options in form of cards
- Each card of dish will display information about that dish including images, videos and texts, etc.
- SignUp/Login to personalize their selections and save for later usages.
